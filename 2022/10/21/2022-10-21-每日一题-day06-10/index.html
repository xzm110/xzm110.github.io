每日一题 day06/10

今天先证明一个定理，再用来做一个小题目。
或许我应该复习一点概率理论了，我常常因为自己菜而感到难过，也因为自己理论基础不扎实而难过。

假设有一列独立同分布的随机变量 $X_1, X_2, X_3…$, 同时 $N(t)$ 是一个强度为 $\lambda$ 的与 $X_i$独立的 Poisson process, 定义 $X(t) = \sum_{i=1}^{N(t)} X_i$ 是一个 compound Poisson process.

Assume $EX_i^2 < \infty$, proof $EX(t) = \lambda t EX_1, VarX(t) = \lambda t EX_1^2$.

Proof it by take condition on $N(t)$,
$$
\begin{align}
EX(t) &= E\left[ EX(t) | N(t) \right] \\
&= E\left[ N(t) EX_1 \right] \\
&= \lambda t EX_1 \\
\end{align}
$$
To calculate $VarX(t)$, we use the decomposition of $VarX(t)$ by taking condition on $N(t)$.
$$
\begin{align}
VarX(t) &= Var\left[ E\left[ X(t) | N(t) \right] \right] + E Var\left[ X(t)| N(t) \right] \\
&= \lambda t (EX_1)^2 + E N(t) VarX_1 \\
&= \lambda t EX_1^2
\end{align}
$$
as desired.

Another proof is using the moment generating function.
$$
\begin{align}
Ee^{sX(t)} &= E\left[Ee^{s\sum_{i=1}^{N(t)} X_i} | N(t)\right] \\
&= E\left[ \left(Ee^{sX_1}\right)^{N(t)} \right] \\
&= e^{ \lambda t\left(Ee^{sX_1}-1\right)} \\
\end{align}
$$
Take derivates of the moment generating function $Ee^{sX(t)}$ with respect to $s$ and set $s=0$, then the result is derived.

A small example:
Assume $X_i$ is multinomial distributed with probability $P(X_i=1) = \frac18, P(X_i = 2) = \frac18, P(X_i=3) = \frac12, P(X_i=4) = \frac14$, and rate $\lambda = 3$
Calculate $EX(10)$
Pretty easy $EX_1 = \frac{23}8$. Hence, $EX(10) = 10 \times 3 \times \frac{23}8 = \frac{345}4$.
