Assume {N(t)}\{ N(t) \}{N(t)} is a Poisson process with rate λ\lambdaλ, evaluate EN(t)N(t+s),0<t,sEN(t) N(t+s), 0 < t, sEN(t)N(t+s),0<t,s.

Solve:

\begin{align} EN(t)N(t+s) &= EN(t)\left(N(t+s) - N(t) \right) + EN(t)^2 \\\\ &= EN(t) E\left(N(t+s) - N(t) \right) + EN(t)^2 \\\\ &= EN(t) EN(s) + EN(t)^2 \\\\ &= \lambda t \lambda s + Var(N(t)) + \left(EN(t)\right)^2 \\\\ &= \lambda^2 ts + \lambda t + \lambda^2 t^2 \\\\ &= \lambda^2 t(t+s) + \lambda t \end{align}

The second equation is a result of independent increments of Poisson porcess.
