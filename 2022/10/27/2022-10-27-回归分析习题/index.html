回归分析习题

  1. For regression with intercept hat matrix $H = X(X^TX)^{-1}X^T$, proof $\frac1n \le h_{ii} \le 1$
    (hint: proof $I-H, H-\frac1n 1^T_n1_n$ is semi definite, $H - \frac1n 1^T_n 1_n$ is semi definite using the intercept)

see Diagonal elements of the projection matrix

  1. 在满足 Guass-Markov 条件的一元线性回归中，对变量 $X, Y$ 做中心化处理，
    推导误差项 $\epsilon$ 的方差 $\sigma^2$ 的无偏估计。
    (hint: 这就相当于 $y = \beta_1 x + \epsilon$)
  1. proof SSR is independent with SSE when normal hypothesis is true
    (hint: note that $SSE = y^T(I-H)y, SSR = y^T (H - \frac1n 1^T_n 1_n)y$, it suffices to proof $(I-H)y$ is independent with $(H-\frac1n 1^T_n 1_n) y$).
    (hint2: $SSE$ is independent with $\beta$, and express $SSR$ by $\beta$).
