When reading the courseware num_lin_alg-newl.pdf of optimization theory, I find the interesting formula.

(A+BC)−1=A−1−A−1B(I+CA−1B)−1CA−1(A + BC)^{-1} = A^{-1} - A^{-1}B(I + CA^{-1}B)^{-1} CA^{-1} (A+BC)−1=A−1−A−1B(I+CA−1B)−1CA−1

and apply

(AB0−I−CA−1B)(xy)=(b−CA−1b)\begin{pmatrix} A & B \\ 0 & -I - CA^{-1}B \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} x \\ y \\ \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} b \\ -CA^{-1}b \\ \end{pmatrix} (A0​B−I−CA−1B​)(xy​)=(b−CA−1b​)

{(A+BC)x=b(I+CA−1B)Cx=CA−1b\begin{cases} (A+BC)x &=b \\ (I+CA^{-1}B)Cx &= CA^{-1}b \end{cases} {(A+BC)x(I+CA−1B)Cx​=b=CA−1b​
