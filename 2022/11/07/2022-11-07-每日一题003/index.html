From 李贤平 chapter5

check ξn\xi_nξn​ satisfy the law of large nuber

P(ξn=log⁡(k))=P(ξn=−log⁡(k))=1/2P\left(\xi_n = \sqrt{\log(k)}\right) = P\left(\xi_n = -\sqrt{\log(k)}\right) = 1/2P(ξn​=log(k)​)=P(ξn​=−log(k)​)=1/2 , ξn\xi_nξn​ is i.i.d.

It suffices to check Markov condition

\begin{align} Var\frac{\sum_{i=1}^n \xi_i}{n} &= \frac1{n^2} \sum_{i=1}^nVar\xi_i \\ &= \frac{\sum_{i=1}^n\log(i)}{n^2} \\ &\le \frac{n\log(n)}{n} \\ &\to 0. \end{align}
